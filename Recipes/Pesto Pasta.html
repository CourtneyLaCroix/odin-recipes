Pesto Pasta

Pasta is an easy, quick and filling meal - that can be paired with many different sauces and various ingredients to make a wide array of dishes. If you're looking to switch up a weekly meal, try out this pesto pasta recipe!

  • 1lb Pasta shape of choice
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 cup pesto
  • 1 cup grape tomatoes
  • 1 lemon
  • Broccoli
  • Zuchinni

Steps to cook

  1. Boil water
  2. flavor water with salt
  3. add pasta when brought to a boil - cook as directed on box
  4. roast Broccoli, Zuchinni and grape tomatoes at 425 degrees for 15-20 minutes
  5. drain water, add noodles back to the pot
  6. add pesto
  7. squeeze lemon juice into the pesto pasta
  8. throw in oven roasted veggies
  9. enjoy!
